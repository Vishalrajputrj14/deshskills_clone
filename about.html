About DASH Infotech

DASH Infotech is a premier institution working in the field of training and education for almost two decades. We aim to build technology-based alliances that empower youth for their career growth and development.

We collaborate with private, government, and corporate agencies to offer short-term and vocational skill training programs. DASH Infotech has a strong presence in Rajasthan, Uttar Pradesh, and parts of Uttarakhand through its network of own and partner centers operating in Bharatpur, Karoli, Dholpur, Udaipur, Jaipur, Bijnor, Moradabad, and Dehradun.

Incorporated: 1997 (Partnership firm) | Key Functionary: Dinesh Kumar
