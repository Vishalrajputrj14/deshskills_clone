üë®‚Äçüè´ About DASH InfoTech

DASH InfoTech is an Authorized Service Provider (SP) of Rajasthan Knowledge Corporation Ltd. (RKCL), Govt. of Rajasthan.

We support more than 165+ training centers across Bharatpur, Alwar, Dhaulpur, Karauli & Dausa districts, facilitating implementation of computer courses and government-sponsored IT programs.

üìö About RKCL

RKCL (Rajasthan Knowledge Corporation Limited) is a Public Limited Company formed as a joint venture of:

  • Government of Rajasthan
  • University of Rajasthan, Jaipur
  • Maharana Pratap University of Agriculture & Technology, Udaipur
  • Vardhaman Mahaveer Open University, Kota
  • RajComp Info Services Ltd.
  • Centre for e-Governance

The main objective is to promote IT-enabled education, improve digital literacy and connectivity, and implement high-quality educational frameworks across Rajasthan. RKCL bridges coordination between students, institutions, and the government with financial & academic support systems.

üéØ Our Objective

DASH InfoTech aims to develop a new educational framework for IT skills in the 21st century in Rajasthan. The mission is to enhance connectivity, promote IT-enabled programs, improve computer skills, and boost coordination among institutions, universities, students, and the government. It also seeks institutional and financial support for present and future programs.
