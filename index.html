Who We Are

DASH Info Tech is a leading IT organization working with a vision to build an IT alliance for global change that would empower youth for development. Since 1997 (Reg. No. 13/05/97), DASH has been delivering short-term skill and long-term vocational training programs across government, private, and corporate sectors.

Partnering with APTECH Ltd since inception, DASH has trained over 25,000 students at its own campuses and 1.5 lakh+ via channel partners in Rajasthan and Uttar Pradesh. Courses have been successfully delivered in cities like Bharatpur, Karauli, Dholpur, Udaipur, Jaipur, Bijnor, Moradabad, and Dehradun.

We are an authorized Service Provider (SP) of RKCL, facilitating 125+ IT Gyan Kendras for implementing various government-sponsored IT programs.

As part of our CSR efforts with DISHA Foundation, we work across districts in Rajasthan and U.P. to uplift youth via skill and livelihood training programs. Notable projects include:

  • SECC-11 Census (covered over 1 crore population)
  • National Population Register project in UP
  • Trained 1200+ youth with RSLDC (Govt. of Rajasthan)
  • BPL Census-2002 and EDP/ESDP programs for SC/ST & BPL students
  • Strong placement network with IT and ITES firms

Geographical Reach: Rajasthan, Uttar Pradesh, Uttarakhand, Chhattisgarh, and Odisha.
